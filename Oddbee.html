Bee Vision

How can we help you?

At Oddbee we care about our customers and their customers. We don’t just come back to you with a template proposal with checkboxes for various bells and whistles.

We start a conversation and we listen.

We interview our clients so we can understand the challenges that they face and how they plan to deal with these challenges. We poke and prod because you never know what lies beneath the surface. We want to know your business and how you work because if there’s anybody that knows your business best, it’s you.

With this information we can create a solution that is tailored to your needs. We can help you define your image, and how you engage your customers online.

We care about creating brand experiences that will inspire and engage your customers.

Holistic Approach

No designs or websites can survive in a vacuum. We refuse to create “just a quick design”, or “a little website” with no relation to your real world challenges.

We live in a new reality, the information revolution has already taken place.

Just like radio and television have become part of daily life for previous generations, always-on internet and mobile devices have become part and parcel of the millennial culture. The border between the online and offline worlds no longer exists: the online and offline worlds are no longer separate but are facets of our one world.

We offer our clients a way to integrate their online and offline presence, a total digital solutions that includes brand development, brand design, web and mobile development, and site support services. We create our digital solutions to match the real world needs of our clients, and spend time and effort in understanding our clients’ business challenges, so we can prepare them for their online audiences.

We create solutions for the internet and the real world.

  • Marketing

    “Know who you’re selling to”

    Who is your target market? How can you spark interest? How do you sell the value of your products and services without sounding like a used car salesman? What defines your brand?

    Marketing is relationship building. You start with a confident hello, hold a meaningful conversation, seal the deal with a handshake, and leave a lasting impression. The top brands in the world evoke interest, exude confidence, and foster long-lasting relationships built on trust. We stick with those brands because they give us the same visceral emotions that the best relationships give us: excitement, security, trust, and love.

    So how do you get there? It's not enough to put your dreams out there and hope someone understands you (after all, your brand is not an online dating profile, and we're not dating coaches). Your brand must be tailored to speak the language of your target market. Your brand is both your first impression, and the lasting image of your company. Your brand is your company's enticing smile its confident poise, and that firm handshake.

    At Oddbee we team up with top marketing professionals, so we can help you with your marketing and branding needs. Whether you're launching a new brand, doing a brand makeover, or expanding your market, we can do the research and analysis, the brand building and positioning so you can win them over with confidence.

    Catch their eye, connect at a deeper level, share beautiful moments, make them want more. It's all about love baby!
